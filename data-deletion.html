Account & Privacy

Request Data Deletion

If you connected your Facebook or Instagram account to MenuOS and want us to delete all data associated with your account, submit the form below. We will process your request within 30 days.

What will be deleted

Social account tokens All OAuth access tokens connecting your Meta account to MenuOS.
Cached analytics data Reach, impressions, and other insights fetched from your Facebook / Instagram accounts.
Your MenuOS account Profile information, content plans, and any other data tied to your account.

Submit deletion request

Prefer to email? Send a request to jose@restaurantdigital.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and your account email. We'll confirm receipt within 2 business days.
