Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2025  ·  Effective immediately

1. Introduction

MenuOS ("we," "our," or "us") is a content management platform designed to help restaurant businesses plan, organize, and analyze their social media content. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our application, including data obtained through connected social media platforms such as Meta (Facebook and Instagram).

By using MenuOS, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account data: name, email address, and credentials used to log in to MenuOS.
  • Connected platform data: when you connect your Facebook Page or Instagram Business Account, we request access to analytics such as reach, impressions, engagement, and follower metrics for pages you manage.
  • Content data: dishes, photos, descriptions, and publication plans you create inside MenuOS.
  • Usage data: log data, browser type, IP address, and in-app actions, collected automatically for performance and security purposes.

We do not collect personal data of your restaurant's end customers, nor do we access private messages or personal profiles of Facebook/Instagram users.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate the MenuOS platform and its features.
  • Display analytics and performance metrics from your connected social accounts.
  • Improve application functionality and user experience.
  • Communicate service updates, security alerts, or support responses.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising targeting.

4. Meta Platform Data

MenuOS uses the Meta Graph API to retrieve performance data from Facebook Pages and Instagram Business Accounts that you explicitly connect and authorize. The data accessed includes:

  • Post reach and impressions
  • Engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares)
  • Follower growth data
  • Page and profile insights available through Meta's API

This data is used solely to display analytics within your MenuOS dashboard. We comply with Meta's Platform Terms and Meta's Developer Policies.

You can revoke MenuOS's access to your Meta accounts at any time by visiting your Facebook App Settings and removing MenuOS from the connected apps list.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: infrastructure, hosting, or analytics vendors that process data on our behalf under confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements: if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and content data for as long as your account is active. Social media analytics data fetched from Meta is stored temporarily for dashboard display and is not retained longer than 12 months. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 8).

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS), access controls, and secure token storage for OAuth credentials. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your credentials confidential.

8. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To submit a deletion request:

  • Use the in-app account deletion feature (when available), or
  • Email us at jose@restaurantdigital.com with subject "Data Deletion Request."

We also provide an automated Data Deletion Request page for users who connected their Meta account to MenuOS.

We will process all requests within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

MenuOS is intended for business users and is not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify active users of material changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of MenuOS after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
